Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

Introduction

Online Now ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent: Online Now does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Network credentials or passwords
  • Browsing history or app usage data
  • Any data that could identify you personally

How the App Works

Online Now monitors your device's network connectivity status using Apple's built-in Network framework. This includes:

  • Connection Status: Whether your device is connected to the internet
  • Connection Type: WiFi, Cellular, or Ethernet
  • VPN Status: Whether a VPN tunnel is active
  • Network Quality: Latency and speed measurements

All this information is processed and displayed locally on your device only. Nothing is transmitted to any server.

Local Data Storage

The App stores your connection history locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time through the app or by deleting the app
  • Is not backed up to iCloud (app data only)

Third-Party Services

Online Now does NOT use any third-party services for:

  • Analytics or crash reporting
  • Advertising or marketing
  • Social media integration
  • Any form of data collection or tracking

Network Requests

The App may make the following network requests for functionality purposes only:

  • Reachability Check: A small request to verify internet connectivity (e.g., to detect captive portals)
  • Speed Test: Download test data to measure connection speed (only when you initiate a speed test)

These requests do not transmit any personal or device information.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for use by people of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over any local data stored on your device through the app's settings or by uninstalling the app.
